About SPAR

SPAR is the world's leading voluntary food retail chain.

The business started with one Dutch store in 1932 and now comprises more than 13,900 stores in over 48 countries on four continents.

Spar's success story began in 1991 when they started with just one retail store.
Today, There are more than 68 altogether across Slovenia.

Spar has been using Spica's time management and access control system, Time&Space, since 1993.
