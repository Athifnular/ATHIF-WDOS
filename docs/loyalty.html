About Loyalty

Bronze Tier

  • Benefits:
    Customers in the Bronze tier typically receive basic benefits such as accumulating points for discounts on future purchases. They may also have access to a monthly newsletter subscription, which keeps them informed about special offers, new products, and upcoming events.

  • Requirements:
    Bronze tier membership is often open to all customers who sign up for the loyalty program.

Silver Loyalty

  • Benefits:
    Customers in the Silver tier usually receive more benefits compared to the Bronze tier, including a higher discount rate on purchases. They may also receive additional perks such as a birthday discount voucher or access to exclusive sales events.

  • Requirements:
    Silver tier membership may require customers to reach a certain spending threshold or accumulate a specific number of loyalty points.

Gold Loyalty

  • Benefits:
    Gold tier members enjoy the highest level of benefits, including the highest discount rate on purchases. They may receive exclusive benefits such as access to special events, personalized offers, and priority customer service.

  • Requirements:
    Gold tier membership usually requires customers to achieve a significant spending level or accumulate a large number of loyalty points within a specified period.
